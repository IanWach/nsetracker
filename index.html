Strategy Notes
PHASE 1 — ACCUMULATE
Buy within the floor range when the counter enters the pre-announcement window. Price anchors tend to be strong here as institutions quietly position.
PHASE 2 — RIDE MOMENTUM
After the results announcement, retail enthusiasm drives the price toward the target. Hold through to AGM date for maximum capture.
PHASE 3 — EXIT & SWAP
Sell into post-AGM strength before dividend exhaust. Use the Swap Calculator to immediately redeploy into the next cycle counter.
